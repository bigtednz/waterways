# Run A1 - Draw for Places

## Overview

**Run Type Code:** A1  
**Name:** Draw for Places  
**End Condition:** Final strike of second target

## Roles

### Role 1: Delivery & Branch Operator

**Label:** Delivery & Branch Operator

#### Actions
1. Take a delivery hose
2. Couple delivery hose into delivery outlet on appliance
3. Establish delivery to first target
4. Couple branch
5. Operate branch to break first target
6. Break out branch after first target is broken
7. Become branch operator on extension
8. Couple branch to extension
9. Operate branch to hit second target (first strike)
10. After burst length is replaced, operate branch to hit second target again (final strike)

#### Orders
- **WATER_ON_DELIVERY** - After branch is coupled at first target
- **WATER_OFF_DELIVERY** - After first target is broken
- **WATER_ON_DELIVERY** - After branch is coupled on extension

#### Notes
- No WATER OFF DELIVERY is given after the final strike
- All delivery orders are given by Number 1

---

### Role 2: Feeder / Assistant Branch / Replacement Runner

**Label:** Feeder / Assistant Branch / Replacement Runner

#### Actions
1. Take feeder hose
2. Couple feeder hose to pump
3. Couple feeder hose to standpipe once flushed
4. May open feeder inlet valve if fitted
5. Assist branch operator at first target
6. After first target is broken, take delivery from Number 4
7. Couple delivery to establish extension
8. Return to pump area
9. After second target is broken, take replacement hose
10. Give male coupling to pump operator
11. Replace burst length and connect couplings

#### Orders
- **WATER_ON_FEEDER** - After feeder is coupled to pump
- **WATER_ON_DELIVERY** - After burst length is replaced

#### Notes
- Feeder must not be connected to standpipe until standpipe is flushed
- Replacement hose operation occurs after second target is broken the first time

---

### Role 3: Pump Operator

**Label:** Pump Operator

#### Actions
1. Take standpipe
2. Sink standpipe fully down
3. Go to pump
4. May open feeder inlet valve if fitted
5. Open delivery valve for first target
6. Close delivery valve after first target
7. Open delivery valve for extension
8. Close delivery valve after second target first break
9. Replace delivery
10. Tie knot in disabled length before target is broken
11. Open delivery valve after replacement

#### Orders
- **WATER_ON_FEEDER** - Repeated from Number 2 (May be repeated at any time)
- **WATER_ON_DELIVERY** - Repeated from Number 1 (Before opening delivery valve)
- **WATER_OFF_DELIVERY** - Repeated from Number 1 (Before closing delivery valve)
- **WATER_ON_DELIVERY** - Repeated from Number 4 (For extension delivery)
- **WATER_OFF_DELIVERY** - Repeated from Number 4 (After second target first break)
- **WATER_ON_DELIVERY** - Repeated from Number 2 (After burst length replacement)

#### Notes
- Must not touch delivery valve until corresponding order is completed
- Disabled knot must be tied before final delivery

---

### Role 4: Hydrant / Flush / Extension Runner

**Label:** Hydrant / Flush / Extension Runner

#### Actions
1. Take key and bar
2. Clear hydrant
3. Flush standpipe
4. Turn water on to feeder
5. Return to appliance
6. After first target is broken, take extension hose
7. Extend delivery
8. Break out middle coupling ready to replace length

#### Orders
- **WATER_ON_FEEDER** - Repeated from Number 3 (Must be completed before Number 2 connects feeder to standpipe)
- **WATER_ON_DELIVERY** - Relay from Number 1 to Number 3
- **WATER_OFF_DELIVERY** - Once second target is broken

#### Notes
- Standpipe must be flushed before feeder is connected
- Acts as relay for delivery orders during extension

---

## End Condition

The run is complete when the **final strike of second target** is achieved.

---

## Procedure Flow Summary

1. **Setup Phase:**
   - Number 4 clears hydrant and flushes standpipe
   - Number 2 couples feeder to pump
   - Number 1 prepares delivery hose

2. **First Target Phase:**
   - Number 1 establishes delivery and couples branch
   - Number 2 assists at first target
   - Water is turned on and first target is broken
   - Water is turned off after first target

3. **Extension Phase:**
   - Number 4 takes extension hose
   - Number 2 takes delivery from Number 4
   - Extension is established
   - Number 1 operates branch on extension

4. **Second Target Phase:**
   - Second target is hit (first strike)
   - Burst length replacement occurs
   - Final strike of second target completes the run

---

## Key Safety and Procedural Points

- Standpipe must be flushed before feeder connection
- Delivery valve operations must follow order sequence
- Disabled length knot must be tied before final delivery
- All orders must be completed before corresponding valve operations
- No water off delivery is given after the final strike
